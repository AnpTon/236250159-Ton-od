About Us

InvaID started June 1 2024, in the Philippines. It started with the goal to protect the Philippines from the various Invasive Species in it, but with various partners InvaID has grown to the entirety of South East Asia.

To Learn more about our Partners:

  • Department of Environment and Natural Resources: DENR
  • Asia-Pacific Forest Invasive Species Network:APFISN
  • National Fisheries Research and Development Institute:NFRDI
